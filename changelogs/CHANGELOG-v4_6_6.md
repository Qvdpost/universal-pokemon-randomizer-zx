# Changes

## Misc Tweaks

- **NEW FEATURE**: The Randomizer will now default to Alolan Formes of pokemon when randomizing Gen7 rom's.
  - Regular variants are still accessible via the 'Allow Alt Formes' option.
  - The Banned Pokemon Editor Dialog will show the Alolan Pokemon instead of their Regular variant in their respective place in the Dex. (Regular variants are shown in the Alt Formes part of the Dex)
